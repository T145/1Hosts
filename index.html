Quickstart Guide

mini - Level 1: unblocks a number of ads & trackers for in-app rewards, anti-AdBlock, etc.
Lite - Level 2: set & forget, balanced, recommended.
Pro - Level 3: prioritizes safety and privacy e.g blocks graph.facebook.com.
Xtra - Level 4: extremly Xtreme in nature, for experienced users only.

Start with a Lite list first.

  • If Lite is too harsh for your taste, downgrade to mini.
  • If Lite doesn't block enough, upgrade to Pro.

Still wanna block more?
Use Xtra: a highly advanced and extremely edgy list, made for daredevils.
